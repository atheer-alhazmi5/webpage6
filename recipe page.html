CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES RECIPE


this delicious recipe is from Nestle ® Toll House

INGREDIENTS

2 ¼ cups all purpose flour

1tsp baking soda

1tsp salt

200g butter,softened

¾ cup granulated suger

¾ cup brown suger

1 tsb vanilla extract

2 large eggs

cups Semi-Sweet Chocolate chips

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. preheat oven to 180℃
  2. combine flour, baking soda & salt in small bowl.
  3. beat butter, sugar& vanilla in a mixer bowl until creamy.
  4. add eggs, one at a time
  5. gradually add flour mixture
  6. add chocolate chips and mix
  7. bake for 9 to 11 minutes
  8. enjoy !
